==Installation==

Install Floating News Headline either via the WordPress.org plugin directory or by uploading the files to your server.

After activating Floating News Headline, go to plugin menu.

Click Add Floating News Headline and customize your headline.

Publish the Headline and copy Floating News Headline shortcode from the bottom of the add images box and embed shortcode on any Page/Post/Text Widget.

That's it. You're ready to go!
